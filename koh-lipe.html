Koh Lipe, Thailand – 7 Nights

Snorkeling, kayaking, island hopping, walking street night market

✈️ Travel Info

SIN → Langkawi (~S$200 RT), + ferry to Koh Lipe (~S$60 one-way).

🏨 Accommodation

3★ beachfront bungalow/hotel – ~S$85/night; total S$595.

Itinerary (Day‑by‑Day)

Day 1: Arrival in Langkawi, transit to Koh Lipe by speedboat, check-in.
Day 2: Relax at Pattaya Beach, try vegetarian Pad Thai jay.
Day 3: Island hopping tour (Snorkeling at Koh Adang, Koh Rawi).
Day 4: Kayaking and hike to viewpoint.
Day 5: Optional scuba diving day or beach relaxation.
Day 6: Explore Walking Street night market, vegetarian street food tasting.
Day 7: Free day for beach or local spa.
Day 8: Return to Langkawi and fly back.

🥗 Dining

Vegetarian Meal Options:Pad Thai jay, vegetarian green curry, mango sticky rice.

Vegetarian meals ~S$20/day/person (approx S$280 total).

🏝️ Activities & Transfers

  • Speedboats/transfers: S$240
  • Boat tours & kayaking: S$200
  • Optional diving: S$900 (if chosen)
  • Massage & extras: S$100
