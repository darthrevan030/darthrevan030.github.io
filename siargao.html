Siargao, Philippines – 8-Day Itinerary

Surfing, island hopping, and crystal-clear lagoons 🌊🏝️

✈️ Travel Info

Singapore → Siargao (via Cebu/Manila): S$330–350 per person round-trip

Accommodation

3★ Beachfront Resort (~S$70/night)
Total: S$560 for 2 pax (8 nights)

Itinerary (Day‑by‑Day)

Day 1: Arrive at Siargao and Cloud 9 Beach chill | Vegetarian dinner
Day 2: Surf lesson / Stand-Up-Paddleboard | Sunset cruise
Day 3: Island hopping to Naked Island, Daku Island, and Guyam Island.
Day 4: Sugba Lagoon kayaking & swimming
Day 5: Hiking to rock pools and Magpupungko tidal pools. | Spa evening
Day 6: Relax or scuba dive | Café dining
Day 7: ATV ride & waterfall visit
Day 8: Leisure & prep for departure

🥗 Dining

Vegetarian Meal Options:Vegetable lumpia, fresh fruit bowls, local vegetable dishes.

Vegetarian meals ~S$20/day/person (approx S$320 total).

  • Boat trips (shared/private): SGD 100-140
  • Scooter rental for inland areas: SGD 10–20/day (~SGD 50 Total)

  • Island hopping (Daku, Guyam, Naked): SGD 100–120 for 2 pax
  • Sugba Lagoon kayak + entrance: SGD 40–50 for 2 pax
  • Magpupungko rock pools + hike: SGD 25 for 2 pax
  • Optional scuba or ATV tour: SGD 60-100 for 2 pax
