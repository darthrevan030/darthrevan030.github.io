End of Year Chutti Options for Dino & Bunny

Hello my bunny! I have complied a short list of options for us to consider for our end of the year chutti. It is still very much bare bones but once we decide a location we can plan more in detail.

I took the liberty of deciding for a beach holiday. If you wanna do a city holiday, we can can look into those options too. But here are the 5 options around SEA for us to consider.

I figured we don't really wanna go full backpacker and stay in shared hostels or guesthouse, so its all 3 star hotels and above. I ensured that theres as many options for activities as possible in these places while avoiding the places you mentioned.

1. Lombok + Gili Islands (Indonesia) — 9 Days

Flights: SGD 208-224 per person round-trip (average SGD 216)
Accommodation: 3-star beachfront resort in Senggigi (~SGD 105/night; total SGD 840)
Estimated Total Cost (2 pax): SGD 3,092 (~SGD 1,546 per person)

Day-by-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in Lombok, check-in at resort, relax at Senggigi Beach, sunset BBQ dinner.
Day 2: Visit Sasak cultural village for traditional crafts and food. Vegetarian tempeh lunch. Evening beach stroll.
Day 3: Waterfall hiking (Tiu Kelep Waterfall), picnic with vegetarian options. Spa in afternoon.
Day 4: Explore nearby beaches and snorkeling spots. Dinner at local vegetarian-friendly warung.
Day 5: Travel to Gili Trawangan by boat, check into beachfront guesthouse. Sunset hill hike.
Day 6: Snorkeling with turtles and coral reefs around Gili Islands. Vegetarian lunch box.
Day 7: Kayaking and beach day. Optional scuba diving.
Day 8: Explore Gili Air and Gili Meno islands by boat. Beach BBQ dinner.
Day 9: Return to Lombok and fly back to Singapore.

Vegetarian Meal Options:

Tempeh goreng (fried tempeh), vegetarian nasi campur, fresh tropical fruits.

2. Perhentian Islands (Malaysia) — 9 Days

Flights + Transfers: SGD 225-280 to Kuala Terengganu + boat (~SGD 70 transfer)
Accommodation: 3-star beach resort (~SGD 70/night; total SGD 560)
Estimated Total Cost (2 pax): SGD 2,300 (~SGD 1,150 per person)

Day-by-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Fly to Kuala Terengganu, transfer to Perhentian, check-in. Beach relaxation.
Day 2: Explore Long Beach, sunset dinner at vegetarian-friendly café.
Day 3: Snorkeling trip to coral reefs and marine park.
Day 4: Jungle hike to Turtle Beach, wildlife spotting. Vegetarian picnic lunch.
Day 5: Kayaking along the coast, visit small islands nearby.
Day 6: Free day for diving or beach time.
Day 7: Visit local fishing village, cultural immersion.
Day 8: Beach day and sunset BBQ.
Day 9: Return to Kuala Terengganu and fly back.

Vegetarian Meal Options:

Vegetarian rendang, nasi lemak with tempeh, fresh fruit smoothies.

3. Koh Lipe (Thailand) — 8 Days

Flights + Transfers: SGD 200 return Singapore-Langkawi + SGD 60 one-way speedboat
Accommodation: 3-star beachfront hotel (~SGD 85/night; total SGD 595)
Estimated Total Cost (2 pax): SGD 2,595 (~SGD 1,295 per person, excluding diving)

Day-by-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in Langkawi, transit to Koh Lipe by speedboat, check-in.
Day 2: Relax at Pattaya Beach, try vegetarian Pad Thai jay.
Day 3: Island hopping tour (Snorkeling at Koh Adang, Koh Rawi).
Day 4: Kayaking and hike to viewpoint.
Day 5: Optional scuba diving day or beach relaxation.
Day 6: Explore Walking Street night market, vegetarian street food tasting.
Day 7: Free day for beach or local spa.
Day 8: Return to Langkawi and fly back.

Vegetarian Meal Options:

Pad Thai jay, vegetarian green curry, mango sticky rice.

4. Phu Quoc (Vietnam) — 8 Days

Flights: SGD 185-210 round-trip per person
Accommodation: 3-star beachfront resort (~SGD 37/night; total SGD 260)
Estimated Total Cost (2 pax): SGD 1,320 (~SGD 660 per person)

Day-by-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive in Phu Quoc, check-in at resort, relax at Long Beach.
Day 2: Snorkeling at An Thoi archipelago. Vegetarian spring rolls picnic.
Day 3: Hiking in Phu Quoc National Park to waterfalls.
Day 4: Kayaking trip and visit pepper farms.
Day 5: Full island boat tour, visit fishing villages.
Day 6: Vietnamese cooking class focusing on vegetarian dishes.
Day 7: Leisure day: cafés, local markets, beach.
Day 8: Departure.

Vegetarian Meal Options:

Vegetarian pho, fresh vegetable spring rolls, vegan hot pot.

5. Siargao (Philippines) — 8 Days

Flights: SGD 330-350 per person round-trip (usually 1-stop)
Accommodation: 3-star beachfront resort (~SGD 70/night; total SGD 560)
Estimated Total Cost (2 pax): SGD 2,080 (~SGD 1,040 per person)

Day-by-Day Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive Siargao, check-in, beach chill.
Day 2: Surf or paddleboard lessons for beginners.
Day 3: Island hopping to Naked Island, Daku Island, and Guyam Island.
Day 4: Kayaking and swimming at Sugba Lagoon.
Day 5: Hiking to rock pools and Magpupungko tidal pools.
Day 6: Free day or optional scuba diving.
Day 7: ATV tour to waterfalls and scenic spots.
Day 8: Departure.

Vegetarian Meal Options:

Vegetable lumpia, fresh fruit bowls, local vegetable dishes.
