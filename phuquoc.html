Phu Quoc, Vietnam – 8-Day Itinerary

Chill beaches, snorkeling, hiking & Vietnamese cuisine 🍜🌴

✈️ Travel Info

Singapore → Phu Quoc (direct VietJet/Scoot): S$185–210 per person round-trip

Accommodation

3★ Beachfront Resort (Vinholidays Fiesta or similar)
Cost: ~S$37/night → Total: S$260 for 2 pax (7 nights)

Itinerary (Day‑by‑Day)

Day 1: Arrive in Phu Quoc, check-in at resort, relax at Long Beach & Rory’s Bar | Dinner at The Pepper Tree
Day 2: Snorkeling at An Thoi | Dinh Cau Night Market
Day 3: Hiking in Phu Quoc National Park to Tranh Waterfall | Spa or sunset cocktails
Day 4: Kayaking | Visit Pepper farm & fish sauce factory
Day 5: Boat tour to Finger & Turtle Islands | Bonfire
Day 6: Cooking class | Coconut Prison Museum
Day 7: Beach + café crawl | Dinner at Crab House
Day 8: Souvenir shopping & departure
