Lombok + Gili Islands, Indonesia – 8 Nights

Waterfalls, Sasak culture village, hiking, snorkeling, diving

✈️ Flights (SIN → LOP return)

From S$208–224/person; average S$216.

🏨 Accommodation

3★ beachfront resort in Senggigi – approx US $75/night (≈S$105/night), total S$840.

Itinerary (Day‑by‑Day)

Days 1–2: Senggigi – beachside resort; Sempana Beach sunset + BBQ (vegetarian tempeh). Visit Sasak cultural village (Sade or Sukarare, entrance ~S$1).
Days 3–4: Day‑trip to Sendang Gile & Tiu Kelep waterfalls (entrance ~$1); hiking; vegetarian nasi campur lunch; spa/rest day.
Days 5–8: Transfer to Gili Trawangan (~S$25 fast boat); snorkeling with turtles; optional dive intro (US$100); beach cafés with vegetarian bowls; kayaking; cycling.
Day 9: Transfer back to Lombok + fly to Singapore.

🥗 Dining

Breakfast included. Lunch/dinner ~US $12–20/day/person (approx S$320 for two).

🚗 Transport & Activities

  • Private car hire (US $40/day × 4 days ≈ S$220)
  • Fast boats + transfers ≈ S$180
  • Snorkeling/diving ≈ S$220
  • Entry fees & spa ≈ S$100
