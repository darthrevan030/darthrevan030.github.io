Perhentian Islands, Malaysia – 8 Nights

✈️ Travel Info

SIN → TGG + taxi + ferry — flights S$225–280; transfers ~S$70/person.

🏨 Accommodation

3★ beachfront resort – ~S$70/night, total S$560.

Itinerary (Day‑by‑Day)

Days 1–2: Arrive, transfer & relax on beach; vegetarian rendang dinner at sunset.
Day 3: Half‑day snorkeling with turtles; evening vegetarian nasi lemak.
Day 4: Jungle hike to Turtle Beach + viewpoint.
Day 5: Kayaking or dive; rice dessert & veggie smoothies.
Days 6–7: Full‑day boat tour to southern reefs; evenings with bonfire & chill.
Day 8: Optional SCUBA + relaxed beach day with music.
Day 9: Boat → jetty → fly home.

🥗 Dining

Vegetarian‑friendly local cuisine ~S$20/day/person (approx S$320).

🎟️ Activities & Transfers

  • Flights + transfers: S$1,120 total
  • Snorkeling/diving/park fees: S$300
